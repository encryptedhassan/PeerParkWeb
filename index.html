What PeerPark offers

Every feature you need to park with confidence

From real-time maps to escrow-based payments. PeerPark is built end-to-end for Pakistan's urban drivers and homeowners.

🗺️

Live Map Discovery

Find available spots near you on an interactive map powered by OpenStreetMap — with real-time updates.

📅

Instant Booking Engine

Select your timeframe, calculate costs automatically, and lock in your spot before anyone else does.

🏠

Host Listing Management

Homeowners list their driveways with photos, hourly pricing, and calendar availability — all admin approved.

🔔

Real-Time Notifications

Hosts get instant in-app alerts for new bookings. Drivers get confirmations and status updates.

💸

Escrow Payments

Funds are securely held until session completion. Supports EasyPaisa and JazzCash natively.

⭐

Safety Index & Ratings

Every spot is scored 1–10 on security. Mutual ratings build trust between hosts and drivers over time.

Simple process

From searching to parked in minutes

PeerPark's flow is designed to be fast, secure, and intuitive for both drivers and hosts.

01

Sign up & verify

Register with your email, phone, and name. JWT-secured login keeps your account safe.

02

Discover nearby spaces

Open the map — spot markers appear based on your GPS coordinates in under 3 seconds.

03

Book your spot

Choose duration, view the cost, and send a booking request. The host is notified instantly.

04

Host accepts & you pay

Payment is processed via EasyPaisa/JazzCash and held in escrow until session ends.

05

Park, leave, rate

Session completes, funds release. Both parties rate each other to build platform trust.

Platform governance
